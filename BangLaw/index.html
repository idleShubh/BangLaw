WHO ARE WE?


A Bangalore-based web development agency dedicated to helping lawyers build their online presence. Founded by Shubh Srivastava, a passionate developer and student, Banglaw is run by Shubh and his friends. We guarantee professional, high-quality websites, with a team that's as approachable as we are skilled.


OUR MISSION

Shubh founded Banglaw to support his studies while helping legal professionals extend their reach through the power of the web. Our goal is to empower more law firms and independent lawyers to connect with a broader audience, while also creating opportunities for other hardworking students to gain experience and support their education.
